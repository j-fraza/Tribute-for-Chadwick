Chadwick Aaron Boseman: A Tribute

"Every person makes a seed grow. One person comes in and waters it. Another person tills the soil. But you know you are making an impact on the world. You know you are changing somebody's mind."

His Life Timeline:

  • November 29, 1976 - Born in Anderson, South Carolina
  • 1995 - Graduated from T.L. Hanna High School
  • 2000 - Graduated from Howard University with a Bachelor in Fine Arts in Directing
  • 2003 - Received his first television role on an episode of Third Watch
  • 2008 - Became a featured role in the TV series Licon Heights
  • 2008 - Appeared in his first feature film, The Express: The Ernie Davis Story
  • 2010 - Lands a regular role in TV series Persons Unknown
  • 2013 - Starred as Jackie Robinson in the film 42
  • 2013 - Starred in indie film The Kill Hole
  • 2014 - Starred in film, Draft Day
  • 2014 - Starred as James Brown in Get on Up
  • 2015 - Began dating Taylor Simone Ledward
  • 2016 - Starred in Gods of Egypt
  • 2016 - Began to portray as T'Challa/Black Panther in the Marvel Comics movie series within Captain America: Civil War
  • 2016 - Diagnosed with stage III colon cancer (progressed to stage IV pre-2020)
  • 2018 - Headlined Black Panther
  • 2019 - Engaged and married to Taylor Simone Ledward
  • 2019 - Featured in the cast of Netflix war drama film Da 5 Bloods, released on June 12, 2020
  • August 28, 2020 - Deceased at the age of 43

— Barack Obama
